Bespoke Design

The topic

Design of a gift-giving experience for Hallmark. The design was personalized for a single user, travelling lover Dary, with the possibility to scale it to a larger audience.

The design included a physical gift card with a link to digital unwrapping experience. The theme of the design is visiting the 7 world wonders.

AR Card

The physical card has an Augmented Reality functionality that connects the physical world with the digital unwrapping.

The connection is through an app on the user's phone that accesses their camera and starts the experience. The revealing of the gift is happening on top of this card.

Personalized

The user gets a personalized experience, by showing them wonders they have already visited and slowly approaching the gift reveal - visiting their next wonder. The gift is then memorable with its engagement and unique presentation.

The interaction

The user interacts with the digital world and a digital guide on their card, they receive congratulating recordings from the gift givers and ultimately are presented with their destination.
