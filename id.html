BIOS interaction design

The topic

The project is the interaction and UX design of a website for a new fictional cinema chain 'BIOS' in the Netherlands. The design process included research of users, sitemapping and wireframing.

Sitemap

After research, the results were transformed into flowcharts and sitemap.

Wireframes

Wireframes made in Adobe XD, demonstrate user's interaction with the website. Data gained from user research was the foundation for labeling and positioning of site elements.

Enhanced UX

The Matchmaker function brings something new to a cinema website and creates an engaging personalized experience.
