TEDxYouth @Bratislava

The topic

#nofilter relates to the fact that today's youth is looking at the world around them through the filters of internet and media. The conference aimed at uncovering these filters and showing what lies beneath.

Pre-conference visuals

First visuals for advertising the conference used the X from TEDx as a way to uncover the filters.

Product design

Product design of shirts, bags, brochures, posters and other print material.

Digital materials

The digital materials used a "scratched off" filter - used for speakers' graphics, brochures, timetables and other digital products.
